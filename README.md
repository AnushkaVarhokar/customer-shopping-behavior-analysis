# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

> An End-to-End Data Analytics Project using **Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI** to analyze customer purchasing behavior, identify business trends, and provide actionable insights for improving sales and customer engagement.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Understanding customer shopping behavior is essential for businesses to improve sales performance, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty.

In this project, customer transaction data was analyzed to identify purchasing patterns across different demographics, product categories, subscription status, and shopping preferences. The project demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow, from data preparation to interactive dashboard development.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand its customers' shopping behavior to answer questions such as:

- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which customer segments contribute the most sales?
- Do subscribed customers spend more?
- Which shipping and payment methods are most preferred?
- How can customer insights improve marketing strategies?

The goal is to transform raw customer data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Clothing generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
- Young customers contributed the highest sales and revenue.
- Approximately 73% of customers were non-subscribers.
- The average purchase amount was **$59.76**.
- The average customer review rating was **3.75**.
- Accessories ranked as the second-best performing category.
- Interactive filters allow detailed customer behavior analysis.

---

## ✅ Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing campaigns on young customers.
- Increase inventory for Clothing and Accessories.
- Expand the customer subscription program.
- Improve customer satisfaction by analyzing product reviews.
- Use customer segmentation for personalized marketing campaigns.
